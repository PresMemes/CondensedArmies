# For Stellaris 4.4.X 'Pegasus'
Condensed Armies is a (relatively) small mod that aims to make dealing with fortress worlds slightly less tedious (Looking at you, Phanon Vault).
# Features
Condensed Armies adds duplicates of existing armies with most of their stats multiplier by x10/x100.
## What stats are multiplied?
- Health
- Damage
- Morale
- Morale Damage
- Collateral Damage
- War Exhaustion
- Cost
- Upkeep
- Production
Recruitment/Build Time is unchanged as waiting 25 years for a single x100 Assault Army is a bit much.
## What armies get Condensed variants?
Generally speaking:
- Can't be a defensive or occupation army
- No unique mechanics (e.g. only 3 per empire or only spawned through events)
Then it'll probably get added to Condensed Armies' roster.
## How do I build them?
Currently three methods:
1. Edicts -> Condensed Armies Logistics Manager
2. Console -> `event pmca_events.999`
3. Dynamic Mod Menu -> Condensed Armies Logistics Manager
## Does the AI build them?
Yes. This can be disabled in the mod menu.
Caveat: The AI does need a pretty strong economy to consider building Condensed Armies, so you probably won't see any unless you're super late game and/or on higher difficulties.
Caveat #2: Nomadic AI empires are currently not allowed to build Condensed Armies due to bureaucratic issues.
## How's the performance?
On Grand Admiral with a default medium galaxy:
Of the 128.088 seconds it took to go from 2340.03.11 to 2341.03.11, Condensed Armies took up ~0.341% of that (7.315 milliseconds).

Anywhere should be fine. Load any submods after Condensed Armies.
